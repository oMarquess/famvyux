About Luper

At Luper, we believe in the power of movies to bring families together. Our mission is to create a community where parents can share honest movie reviews and ratings, helping each other make informed decisions about what films are suitable for their children. We combine the vast database of IMDB with the real-world experiences of parents to provide a unique perspective on movie content.

  • Community-Driven Ratings

    Our platform is built on the contributions of parents worldwide, offering genuine insights into movie suitability for different age groups.

  • Extensive Movie Database

    With access to IMDB's comprehensive movie database, Luper provides detailed information and ratings for a vast array of films.

  • Family-Friendly Focus

    Our focus is on creating a safe, family-friendly environment where parents can find and discuss films that align with their values and preferences.
